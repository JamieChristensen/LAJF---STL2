Environment objects: Terrain, obstacles, background and the like - anything that is a prefab and can be seen in the scene (yet isn't part of the main moving objects)

Enemies: Any threats to player1 that have agency

Managers and utilities: Prefabs that facilitate flow between higher-level game-states.

Gods: God-related prefabs (Players 2, 3 and 4)

Player: Player prefab, and anything that doesn't fit other categories but are related to the player.

UI: User interface prefabs, menus and the likes.
